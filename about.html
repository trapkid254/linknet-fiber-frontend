About Linknet Fiber

Kenya's fastest growing fiber internet service provider

Our Mission

At Linknet Fiber, we're on a mission to connect every Kenyan home and business with world-class fiber internet. We believe that fast, reliable internet is not a luxury—it's a necessity for modern life, education, and business.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Linknet Fiber began with a simple observation: Kenyans deserve better internet. While the world was moving towards gigabit speeds, many Kenyans were still struggling with unreliable connections and data caps.

Today, we've grown to serve thousands of customers across Kenya's major cities, with plans to expand to every county by 2026. Our pure fiber network delivers symmetrical speeds, unlimited data, and 99.9% reliability.

Our Values

  • Customer First: Every decision we make starts with our customers' needs.
  • Innovation: We constantly upgrade our network and services.
  • Reliability: We build our network to work when you need it most.
  • Transparency: No hidden fees, no confusing contracts.
