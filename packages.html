Frequently Asked Questions

What is the installation process?

Our technicians will visit your location to install the fiber optic cable and set up your WiFi router. Installation typically takes 2-3 hours and is completely free.

Are there any data caps?

No, all Linknet Fiber packages come with truly unlimited data. There are no caps, throttling, or fair usage policies.

What equipment is provided?

We provide a high-performance WiFi 6 router free of charge. This router is optimized for fiber connections and can handle multiple devices simultaneously.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes! You can upgrade your package at any time. The change will be effective immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.
